What We DO

Guangde Huachang New Material Co., Ltd. is a science and technology enterprise integrating scientific research, development, production and sales of insulating materials. The company is located in Guangde Economic and Technological Development Zone of Anhui Province, adjacent to Zhejiang and Jiangsu,

The company's products are: FR4/FR5 insulation board, G10/G11 insulation board, anti-static board, Yinggang board, synthetic stone, non-halogen board, prepreg, and insulation board processing parts.
